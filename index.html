Why SCADA?

SCADA allows teams to define robust and repeatable extract-transform-load processes from various sources into an index. Resources are enriched using distributable tasks that provide informative reporting and monitoring. Before exiting the pipeline, resources are validated to provide quality assurance for downstream consumers.
